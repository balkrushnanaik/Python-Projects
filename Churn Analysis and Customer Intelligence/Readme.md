# 📊 Business Insights

## Overall Customer Churn
- The overall customer churn rate is **28.57%**, while the retention rate is **71.43%**.
- Although the majority of customers continue using the service, nearly **3 out of every 10 customers** have churned, indicating opportunities to improve customer retention.
- Reducing churn by even a small percentage can significantly improve long-term revenue and customer lifetime value.

---

## Plan Type Analysis
- **Basic Plan** customers recorded the highest churn rate (**60%**), making it the most vulnerable subscription plan.
- **Premium Plan** customers showed the lowest churn rate (**14.29%**), indicating higher customer satisfaction and loyalty.
- **Standard Plan** maintained a moderate churn rate (**22.22%**).

### Business Insight
The Premium plan appears to deliver better value to customers. The company should investigate why Basic plan users leave by analyzing pricing, available features, service quality, and customer expectations. Introducing additional benefits or targeted upgrade offers could help reduce churn among Basic users.

---

## State-wise Churn Analysis
- Karnataka recorded a **100% churn rate**, meaning every customer from this region discontinued the service.
- Meghalaya also experienced a high churn rate (**66.67%**).
- Telangana reported a churn rate of **50%**.
- States such as Maharashtra, Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, Kathmandu, and Nagaland maintained **0% churn**, indicating strong customer retention.

### Business Insight
Customer experience differs significantly across regions. Karnataka requires immediate investigation to identify issues related to service availability, customer support, pricing, or competition. Best practices from low-churn states should be replicated in high-risk regions.

---

## Customer Acquisition Channel Performance
- **Organic customers** achieved a **0% churn rate**, making them the most loyal customer segment.
- **Paid customers** experienced a relatively low churn rate (**16.67%**).
- **Referral customers** showed the highest churn rate (**83.33%**).

### Business Insight
Organic acquisition attracts highly engaged and loyal customers. Referral campaigns should be reviewed to ensure that incentives are attracting quality customers rather than short-term users who leave quickly.

---

## Revenue Analysis
- The Average Revenue Per User (ARPU) is **18.85**.
- Revenue at risk due to churn is approximately **73.94 (Rs 'K')**.

### Business Insight
Customer churn is directly impacting business revenue. Retaining existing customers, especially those with higher subscription values, can substantially reduce revenue loss and improve profitability.

---

## Customer Lifetime Analysis
- The average customer tenure is approximately **1,492 days**, indicating that many customers remain with the company for several years.

### Business Insight
The business has a strong long-term customer base. The primary objective should be preventing existing loyal customers from leaving rather than focusing solely on acquiring new customers.

---

## Customer Support Analysis
- The escalation rate is **19.05%**.
- Customers submit an average of **0.43 complaints per user**.

### Business Insight
Although complaint frequency is relatively low, nearly one-fifth of support requests require escalation. Improving first-level support resolution can enhance customer satisfaction while reducing operational costs.

---

## Escalation vs Churn Relationship
- The correlation between escalation and churn is **0.77**, indicating a strong positive relationship.

### Business Insight
Customers with escalated support cases are significantly more likely to churn. Reducing escalations through faster response times and better issue resolution should become a key retention strategy.

---

## Churn Risk Prediction
Customers were categorized into **Low** and **High** churn-risk groups using a churn score.

### Business Insight
High-risk customers can be proactively targeted through personalized retention campaigns, loyalty rewards, discount offers, or dedicated customer support before they decide to leave.

# 🎯 Recommendations

- Improve the value proposition of the Basic subscription plan.
- Investigate the reasons behind Karnataka's exceptionally high churn rate.
- Review and optimize the referral acquisition program to improve customer quality.
- Proactively engage high-risk customers before they cancel.
- Reduce customer support escalations through faster issue resolution.
- Continue investing in organic acquisition channels since they produce the most loyal customers.
- Monitor churn trends regularly using dashboards and KPI reports to enable timely business decisions.
